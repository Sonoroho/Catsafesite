What to do in an emergency

The toxicity of plants differs between species so being aware of them could potentially save your cat's life. If you suspect your cat has eaten or come into contact with something particularly toxic, it is best to contact your veterinarian as soon as possible. Just like with human emergencies, quick action is key.

Note: It can be helpful to note how much of the substance and when it was ingested if possible. It can help the vet track symptoms and severity.
