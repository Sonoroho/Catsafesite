Toxic plants to watch out for

There are so many plants in our world and a large portion of them are wonderful to look at. Unfortunately, not all of them are safe for our furbabies to ingest or even be around in general. Toxicity can vary depending on the kind of plant or tree the toxin comes from. Some of the toxic plants might surprise you. Christmas Hollies, Birds of Paradise, Calla Lillies, and even apple trees are all toxic to cats. A more comprehensive listing of toxic plants can be found in the useful links section.
